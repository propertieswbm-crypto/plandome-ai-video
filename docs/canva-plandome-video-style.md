# Plandome Canva Video Style Reference

Derived from the Plandome Classic 3D Vertical, Mezzanine Commercial Vertical, and Retargeting Vertical Canva families.

Classic 2.0 topic references also include Commercial Rear Extension, Change of Use, Basement Development, Loft, HMO and Plandome Pro portfolio audit campaigns from the SP folder.

## Narrative rhythm

- Build 7–12 short visual beats for a typical social advert.
- Keep display copy to 2–6 words wherever possible.
- Use the sequence: hook, problem, consequence, risks, result, Plandome method, Decision Pack, CTA.
- Give every voiceover clause its own synchronized visual beat.
- Reserve a dedicated scene for the Decision Pack product reveal.
- Do not force a Decision Pack into scripts whose offer is a free assessment or portfolio audit.
- Price-led scripts use the investment or avoidable loss as the opening contrast and receive dedicated monetary-risk frames.
- Adapt constraint lists by project type: use class, Article 4, licensing, excavation, drainage, flooding, head height, structure, neighbours, conservation or portfolio development value.

## Visual grammar

- Portrait-first 1080 × 1920 composition.
- Alternate full-frame property imagery, oversized type, checklists and product cards.
- Use cream, charcoal and Plandome orange as the core family. Secondary themes may use muted blue, green, rose or violet while retaining charcoal and a high-contrast accent.
- Use stacked shadows, offset cards and slight rotations for the Classic 3D family.
- Keep the Plandome logo continuously readable in a high-contrast white panel.
- Use line-specific UK property imagery. Never reuse generic historic footage for an unrelated planning, regulation or risk statement.

## Motion

- Use fast word-level entrances followed by one restrained ambient image move.
- Alternate ascend, lateral slide and scale/rotation entrances.
- Use directional cover transitions between every scene.
- Do not animate text out before the transition.
- Synchronize scene boundaries to ElevenLabs character timestamps.

## Product and CTA

- Animate the Decision Pack as a physical stacked pack.
- Avoid explanatory paragraphs on the product scene.
- End with one direct action and the Plandome logo.
